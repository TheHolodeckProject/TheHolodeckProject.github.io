Welcome to the UIUC Holodeck!

Through a collaboration with the Memory Systems Lab, the Decision Neuroscience Lab, and the Lifelong Brain and Cognition Lab at the Beckman Institute at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, (UIUC) the Holodeck project was created with the vision of taking advantage of the latest in off-the-shelf gaming technology in order to create more immersive and ecologically valid environments in which Training and Cognitive Testing could be developed. Through the use of the Oculus Rift, Leap Motion, Microsoft Kinect, and other virtual reality technologies, the Holodeck has become a place where your dreams can become reality.
